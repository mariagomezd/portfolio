Skills

My Technical & Soft Skills

I connect user needs with clear structures and visuals: mapping journeys, creating flows, and turning them into interfaces that feel calm and intuitive.

  • UX research basics (interviews, surveys, simple usability checks)
  • User flows, site maps, and low‑ to high‑fidelity wireframes
  • High‑fidelity UI design for responsive web and mobile
  • Design systems, components, and visual style guidelines

I use a mix of design and visual tools to move from quick ideas to polished prototypes that are ready to share with teams and stakeholders.

  • Figma (components, auto layout, interactive prototypes)
  • Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator for visual assets
  • Basic HTML and CSS to understand layouts and handoff
  • Organizing design files so they are easy to navigate

I like working in an iterative way: sharing early, collecting feedback, and translating it into clear next steps for the product.

  • Iterative design with regular feedback loops
  • Explaining design decisions in a simple, friendly way
  • Collaborating with developers, PMs, and stakeholders
  • Documenting flows and decisions to keep everyone aligned
