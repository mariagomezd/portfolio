About

UX & UI designer with a media design background.

I’m a UX & UI designer with a multimedia design background, passionate about understanding how people think, feel, and move through digital products. I’m especially interested in how we can reduce complexity so that interfaces feel intuitive instead of overwhelming.

About

Design grounded in people, clarity, and calm experiences.

I approach projects by asking questions first: How do users think? What do they actually need? Where do things get confusing? From there, I move into user flows, wireframes, visual systems, and interactive prototypes that connect user needs with clear, purposeful interfaces.

My background in multimedia and visual design helps me balance structure and emotion: I care about hierarchy, rhythm, and pacing just as much as colors and type. I enjoy turning messy ideas into simple journeys that feel calm and understandable.

I’m currently focusing on growing as a UX & UI designer through hands-on projects, collaboration, and continuous learning. I’m excited about opportunities where I can contribute to meaningful products, learn from teammates, and keep improving the way I think about design.
