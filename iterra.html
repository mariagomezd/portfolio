Project

Iterra 🌎

App prototype for trip planning

Overview

About This Project

Go to Case Study

Iterra is a travel app concept designed to simplify planning and make the experience intuitive, clear, and user-centered.

This project focused on exploring how digital tools can help users plan trips without feeling overwhelmed. The final prototype demonstrates a clean, approachable interface that enables users to organize their trips confidently. Although conceptual, the work highlights how human-centered design can reduce complexity and enhance digital experiences, a key goal in today’s travel tech landscape.
